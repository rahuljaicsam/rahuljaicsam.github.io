Frequently Asked Questions

How does compute power donation work?

When you donate compute power, you're allowing our research projects to utilize your unused computational resources. We provide secure software that runs in the background of your device, using resources only when they're not needed by you. The software automatically adjusts based on your usage patterns to ensure it never interferes with your work.

Is my data secure when I donate compute power?

Absolutely. Our compute donation system is designed with security as the top priority. The software only accesses computational resources, never your personal data or files. All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted, and we implement strict isolation between the research workloads and your system.

What kind of research will my donation support?

Your donated compute power supports a wide range of medical research initiatives, including:

  • AI-driven analysis of medical imaging for early disease detection
  • Protein folding simulations for drug discovery
  • Genomic data processing for personalized medicine
  • Medical robotics control systems development
  • Virtual reality medical training simulations

You can specify preferences for which research areas your donation supports.

How can I track the impact of my donation?

Once you register as a compute donor, you'll receive access to a personal dashboard that shows real-time statistics about your contribution, including compute hours donated, research projects supported, and the collective impact of our donor community. We also send monthly impact reports highlighting research breakthroughs made possible by our compute donation network.
