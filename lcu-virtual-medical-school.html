Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our virtual medical school program

How does virtual medical education work?

+

Our virtual medical education combines AI-driven learning platforms, VR simulations, and real-time interaction with faculty. Students access course materials, attend virtual lectures, participate in simulated clinical scenarios, and collaborate with peers through our advanced digital platform.

Are virtual medical degrees accredited?

+

Yes, our virtual medical program is fully accredited by relevant medical education authorities. Our curriculum meets all standard requirements for medical education while incorporating innovative virtual learning methods.

What technology requirements are needed?

+

Students need a reliable high-speed internet connection, a modern computer with webcam, and VR equipment (provided by the school). We provide all necessary software and access to our virtual learning platform.
