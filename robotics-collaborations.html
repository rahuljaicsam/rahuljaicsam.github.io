Collaboration Types

We offer various collaboration models to meet the diverse needs of our partners in the robotics healthcare ecosystem.

Research Partnerships

Join forces with our world-class researchers to tackle complex challenges in medical robotics. Research partnerships provide access to our state-of-the-art facilities, high-IQ talent pool, and interdisciplinary expertise.

Benefits include:

  • Co-authored research publications in prestigious journals
  • Joint intellectual property rights
  • Access to specialized robotics laboratories and equipment
  • Collaboration with our network of medical institutions for clinical validation

Ideal for: Research institutions, R&D departments, and companies focused on breakthrough innovations in healthcare robotics.

Technology Licensing

License our cutting-edge robotics technologies to accelerate your product development and gain a competitive advantage in the healthcare market.

Available technologies include:

  • Precision surgical robotics systems and components
  • Adaptive control algorithms for rehabilitation robots
  • Human-robot interaction frameworks for caregiving applications
  • Sensor fusion technologies for enhanced robot perception

Ideal for: Medical device manufacturers, healthcare technology companies, and startups looking to incorporate advanced robotics into their products.

Industry-Sponsored Projects

Fund specific research initiatives aligned with your organization's strategic goals. Our team will work closely with your experts to deliver tailored solutions to your robotics challenges.

Project types include:

  • Proof-of-concept development for novel robotics applications
  • Optimization of existing robotic systems for specific medical procedures
  • Customization of robotics platforms for specialized healthcare environments
  • Validation studies for regulatory approval processes

Ideal for: Healthcare providers, medical technology companies, and pharmaceutical firms seeking targeted robotics solutions.

Educational Partnerships

Collaborate on training programs to develop the next generation of robotics specialists for healthcare applications. We offer customized educational experiences for your technical teams.

Offerings include:

  • Specialized workshops on medical robotics design and implementation
  • Executive education programs on robotics strategy in healthcare
  • Technical training for clinical staff on robotic system operation
  • Joint development of robotics curricula for healthcare professionals

Ideal for: Hospitals, medical schools, continuing education providers, and companies investing in workforce development.

Collaboration Process

Our streamlined process makes it easy to establish and maintain productive partnerships in robotics innovation.

1

Initial Consultation

Begin with a comprehensive discussion of your organization's goals, challenges, and vision for robotics in healthcare. Our team will help identify potential collaboration opportunities aligned with your strategic objectives.

2

Partnership Design

Work with our collaboration specialists to craft a customized partnership model that addresses your specific needs, timeline, and resource considerations. We'll define clear objectives, deliverables, and success metrics.

3

Agreement Formalization

Establish the legal and operational framework for our collaboration, including intellectual property rights, resource commitments, confidentiality provisions, and governance structure.

4

Project Implementation

Launch the collaborative initiative with dedicated project teams, regular progress reviews, and agile adaptation to emerging challenges and opportunities in the rapidly evolving robotics landscape.

5

Evaluation & Expansion

Assess outcomes against established metrics, document lessons learned, and explore opportunities to expand or evolve our partnership based on achieved successes and emerging priorities.
