Equipment Specifications

Our VR medical training programs utilize state-of-the-art equipment to deliver the most realistic and effective learning experience.

Hardware Components

  • MediVision Pro Headset

    8K resolution per eye, 200° FOV, integrated eye tracking, and spatial audio for complete immersion.

  • TactileSense Gloves

    Haptic feedback with 120 individual actuators per hand, providing realistic tactile sensations for surgical procedures.

  • BodyTrack Sensors

    Full-body tracking system with 16 wireless sensors for precise movement capture and analysis.

  • Instrument Replicas

    Physical replicas of medical instruments with embedded sensors for realistic handling and feedback.

Software Features

  • Adaptive Learning AI

    Machine learning algorithms that adjust difficulty and scenarios based on user performance and learning patterns.

  • Real-time Feedback System

    Instant visual and haptic feedback on technique, precision, and decision-making during procedures.

  • Multi-user Collaboration

    Support for up to 20 simultaneous users in the same virtual environment for team training exercises.

  • Comprehensive Analytics

    Detailed performance metrics, learning progress tracking, and personalized improvement recommendations.

Curriculum Integration

Our VR medical training programs are designed to seamlessly integrate with existing medical curricula at all levels of education.

For Medical Schools

Our VR training modules align with AAMC and LCME standards, providing a structured approach to incorporating virtual reality into medical education. Programs can be customized to match your specific curriculum requirements and learning objectives.

  • Preclinical anatomy and physiology visualization
  • Clinical skills development and assessment
  • Standardized patient interactions
  • Objective structured clinical examination (OSCE) preparation

  • Milestone-based competency assessment

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our VR medical training programs.

Our VR training programs are compatible with a range of VR hardware, including Oculus Quest 2/3, HTC Vive Pro, and Valve Index. For the full experience with haptic feedback, we recommend using our specialized MediVision Pro Headset and TactileSense Gloves, which can be purchased or leased as part of your institutional package. We also offer a complete turnkey solution that includes all necessary hardware, software, and support services.

Our anatomical models are developed using a combination of high-resolution medical imaging data (CT, MRI, and ultrasound), cadaveric studies, and input from leading medical specialists. Each model undergoes rigorous validation by a panel of anatomists and practicing physicians to ensure 99.8% anatomical accuracy. We regularly update our models based on the latest research and feedback from medical professionals.

Yes, customization is one of our core strengths. We work closely with each institution to tailor our VR training modules to their specific curriculum, specialty focus, and learning objectives. Our development team can create custom scenarios, procedures, and assessment metrics based on your requirements. We also offer an SDK for institutions with in-house development capabilities who wish to create their own content within our platform.

Our platform includes comprehensive analytics that track various performance metrics, including procedural accuracy, decision-making speed, error rates, and learning progression over time. We also conduct pre- and post-training assessments to measure knowledge retention and skill transfer. Additionally, we work with institutions to design research studies that evaluate the impact of VR training on clinical outcomes, comparing traditional training methods with our VR-based approach.

We provide comprehensive technical support and training as part of our service package. This includes initial setup and installation, faculty training workshops, ongoing technical support via phone, email, and remote assistance, regular software updates, and access to our online knowledge base and community forum. Our dedicated customer success team works with each institution to ensure smooth implementation and optimal utilization of our VR training programs.
